How We Works

Why To Use this Service?

In the midst of the Covid-19 Pandemic, having a clean and safe place to live or conduct business is a top priority. Our service allows you to feel safe and assured that you, your loved ones, or your customers are spending time in areas free of bacteria and airborne viruses.

Where can this Service be Used?

residential homes, business office, commercial buildings, churches, sport facilities, warehouses, medical office, retirement homes, hotels, restaurant, bars, schools, hospitals

How do we Disinfect?

With the use of our new electrostatic sprayer and fogger equipment we are able to dispense vapor that is made of non-toxic solutions in order to kill off pathogens on surfaces, including ceilings, walls, furniture, and floors. This method of disinfecting is so rapid and safe that you are even able to stay in your place of business or home while the disinfecting happens. All members of our staff come dressed in full hazmat suits, booties and face shields to insure that no pathogens are brought in by our staff.
